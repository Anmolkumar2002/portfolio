PG Life

PG Life is a comprehensive web application designed to simplify the process of managing and finding paying guest accommodations. Users can browse and filter PG listings by location, price, and amenities, explore detailed profiles with images, facilities, and contact information, and even make seamless online bookings. At the same time, PG owners are provided with an admin panel to update availability, manage listings, and interact with tenants more effectively.
